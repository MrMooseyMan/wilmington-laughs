Where to see live comedy in the Port City

Wilmington's Comedy Venues

From dedicated comedy clubs to bars hosting weekly open mics, Wilmington has plenty of places to catch live laughs. Here's your guide to every venue in the Port City comedy scene.

Dead Crow Comedy Room

Wilmington's only full-time comedy club, Dead Crow is the heart of the local scene. National touring headliners perform Thursday through Saturday, with improv and open mics earlier in the week.

Dead Crow Comedy Room

511 N. 3rd Street, Downtown Wilmington

Shows: Wed-Sat | Tickets: $10-$30+

Get Tickets

Laws & Libations

This downtown bar hosts two weekly comedy events: Wednesday open mics and the Thursday Women of Wilmington showcase, featuring all-female stand-up performers.

Laws & Libations

108 Grace Street, Downtown

Shows: Wed & Thu | FREE

Barzarre

Formerly the Juggling Gypsy, this "unusual bar" on Castle Street hosts the longest-running Monday open mic in Wilmington. A laid-back atmosphere perfect for trying new material.

Barzarre

1610 Castle Street

Open Mic: Mondays 6:30 PM | FREE
