Things to Do in Wilmington NC: Live Comedy

Looking for entertainment in the Port City? Catch a comedy show!

Comedy: Wilmington's Best Entertainment

Whether you're a local looking for weekend plans or a visitor exploring the Port City, live comedy is one of Wilmington's best-kept secrets. With shows almost every night of the week, you'll always find something to laugh about.

Date Night Ideas

Skip the movie theater and experience something unique! A comedy show is the perfect date night:

  • Intimate venue - Dead Crow seats about 100, so every seat is a good one
  • Shared experience - Laughing together creates connection
  • Dinner options nearby - Downtown Wilmington has great restaurants within walking distance
  • Affordable - Most shows are $10-$25 per person
