Cape Fear Comedy Festival

Every May, Wilmington transforms into a comedy destination for the Cape Fear Comedy Festival. Hosted primarily at Dead Crow Comedy Room, the festival features multiple days of shows, workshops, and the finals of Port City's Top Comic competition.

What to Expect

  • National Headliners - Touring comedians you've seen on Netflix, Comedy Central, and late night
  • Local Showcases - The best of Wilmington's comedy scene
  • Port City's Top Comic Finals - Watch local comedians compete for the title
  • Improv & Sketch Shows - DareDevil Improv and special performances
  • Workshops - Learn from visiting pros

Port City's Top Comic

Running alongside the festival, the Port City's Top Comic competition crowns Wilmington's funniest comedian. Local and regional comics compete through preliminary rounds leading to the festival finals.
