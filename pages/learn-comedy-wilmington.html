Learn Comedy in Wilmington

Stand-up, improv, and sketch comedy classes for all skill levels

Start Your Comedy Journey

Whether you want to become a stand-up comedian, improve your public speaking, or just have fun, Wilmington offers comedy training for every goal and skill level.

Types of Comedy Classes

Improv Comedy

DareDevil Improv offers a full curriculum from 101 to 404. No experience necessary to start - just bring an open mind and willingness to play.

  • 101: 6 weeks, ~$225
  • 201-404: 8 weeks each, ~$250
  • Graduates perform at Dead Crow Improv Night

Stand-Up Comedy

Look Who's Standing Up! taught by Nancy Witter covers joke writing, timing, and stage presence. Multi-week course culminating in a graduation showcase.

Sketch Writing

Learn to write comedy sketches from instructors trained at Groundlings and UCB.

Kids Programs

DareDevil offers improv workshops for young performers - great for building confidence and creativity.
