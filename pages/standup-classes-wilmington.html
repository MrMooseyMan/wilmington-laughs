Learn Stand-Up Comedy in Wilmington

Ready to try stand-up? Wilmington offers hands-on classes where you'll learn joke writing, stage presence, and timing from experienced comedians who've performed across the country.

Look Who's Standing Up!

Taught by award-winning comedian Nancy Witter, this multi-week course at Dead Crow Comedy Room takes you from concept to stage. You'll learn:

  • How to find your comedic voice
  • Joke structure and writing techniques
  • Stage presence and microphone skills
  • Timing, callbacks, and crowd work basics

The course culminates in a graduation showcase where you'll perform your material for a live audience!

About Nancy Witter

MAC Award winner and Nick at Nite Stand-Up Showdown finalist. Nancy has been teaching comedy in Wilmington for years, helping dozens of students find their voice on stage.

Learn More

What to Expect

  1. Week 1-2: Finding material and writing your first jokes
  2. Week 3-4: Refining material and learning stage craft
  3. Week 5-6: Practice performances and feedback
  4. Graduation: Perform your set at Dead Crow!

After the Class

Once you've graduated, Wilmington has plenty of open mics to keep practicing. Many class alumni become regulars at Dead Crow's Thursday open mic and eventually start booking paid gigs around town.
