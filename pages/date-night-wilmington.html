Best Date Night in Wilmington: Comedy Shows

Dinner and a show? Make it dinner and laughs at Dead Crow Comedy Room

Why Comedy Makes the Perfect Date

Tired of the same dinner-and-a-movie routine? A comedy show is the ideal date night - you'll laugh together, share a unique experience, and have plenty to talk about afterward.

Plan Your Evening

Step 1: Dinner Downtown

Dead Crow Comedy Room is right in the heart of downtown Wilmington. Grab dinner at one of these nearby spots before the show:

  • Boca Bay - Upscale Mediterranean (5 min walk)
  • Caprice Bistro - French cuisine (3 min walk)
  • Circa 1922 - Tapas and cocktails (5 min walk)
  • PinPoint - Farm-to-table (2 min walk)

Step 2: The Show

Arrive 15-20 minutes early to grab drinks and get good seats. Shows typically run 90 minutes to 2 hours. The bar serves beer, wine, and cocktails.

Pro Tip

Friday 7 PM shows tend to be more relaxed. Saturday late shows (9:30 PM) are more energetic and sometimes edgier.

Step 3: After the Show

The show usually ends around 10-10:30 PM - perfect time for a nightcap! Downtown bars are steps away.
