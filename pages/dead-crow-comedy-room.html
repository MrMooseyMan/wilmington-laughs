About Dead Crow Comedy Room

Located at 511 N. 3rd Street in downtown Wilmington, Dead Crow Comedy Room is the Port City's only full-time comedy club. Since opening, it has become the home of Wilmington's comedy scene and a stop for nationally touring comedians.

What to Expect

  • Seating: First-come, first-served general admission
  • Drinks: Full bar with beer, wine, and cocktails
  • Age: 18+ (21+ for late shows)
  • Duration: Shows typically run 90 minutes to 2 hours

Parking

Street parking is available throughout downtown. The deck at Water Street is a short walk away.

Annual Events

Cape Fear Comedy Festival (May) - Multi-day festival with dozens of comedians
Port City's Top Comic (Summer) - Annual competition for local comedians
