Wilmington's Local Comedians

Meet the funny people who make the Port City laugh

Wilmington's Comedy Stars

The Port City has produced and nurtured some seriously funny people. From Netflix specials to late-night appearances, Wilmington comedians are making their mark.

Notable Wilmington Comedians

Cliff Cash

Wilmington native and Netflix Trial by Laughter winner. Cliff filmed his special "The Long Road" at Thalian Hall and continues to tour nationally while calling Wilmington home.

Nancy Witter

MAC Award winner and Nick at Nite Stand-Up Showdown finalist. Nancy not only performs but teaches the next generation through her "Look Who's Standing Up!" classes at Dead Crow.

Wills Maxwell Jr.

Named Port City's Top Comic 2019. Creator of "What Did We Miss?" and StandUp NBC Regional Finalist. Active in the local scene and beyond.

Timmy Sherrill

Known as "The Godfather" of Wilmington comedy. Former Nutt Street owner and Charleston Comedy Festival winner. A veteran who helped build the scene.

Lew Morgante

Runs Gruff Goat Comedy showcases at Waterline Brewing. Regular host at Dead Crow known for his crowd work.

Rising Stars

The open mic scene is full of rising talent: Carson Hennessey, Colleen O'Meara, Julia Desmond, Jack Nelson, Zack Burke, and many more grinding it out every week.

See Local Comics

The best way to catch local talent is at the Thursday open mic at Dead Crow or the Women of Wilmington showcase at Laws & Libations.
