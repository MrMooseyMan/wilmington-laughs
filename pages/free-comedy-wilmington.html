Comedy Doesn't Have to Cost a Thing

Wilmington has one of the best free comedy scenes anywhere. From open mics to improv shows, you can laugh every night of the week without spending a dime on tickets.

Why Free Comedy is Great

  • See future stars - Many famous comedians started at open mics
  • Intimate atmosphere - Small venues mean you're close to the action
  • Support local talent - These comedians are your neighbors
  • Low-pressure entertainment - Come and go as you please

Pro Tip

Dead Crow's Wednesday Improv Night is completely free and features polished performers from DareDevil Improv. It's the best free show in town.

What to Know

While tickets are free, venues may have drink minimums or expect you to purchase from the bar. Budget for a drink or two to support the venue hosting free comedy.
