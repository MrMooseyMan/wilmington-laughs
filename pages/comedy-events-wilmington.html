Wilmington's Thriving Comedy Scene

Wilmington, North Carolina has become a hotspot for live comedy on the East Coast. Whether you're looking for nationally touring headliners, local stand-up showcases, or spontaneous improv performances, the Port City delivers laughs every night of the week.

Where to Find Comedy Events

Dead Crow Comedy Room

Wilmington's only dedicated comedy club hosts shows Thursday through Saturday, featuring touring comedians and local talent. Located at 511 N. 3rd Street in downtown Wilmington, Dead Crow is the heart of the local comedy scene.
