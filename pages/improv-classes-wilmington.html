Learn Improv in Wilmington

Whether you want to perform on stage, improve your public speaking, or just have fun, Wilmington's improv classes offer something for everyone. DareDevil Improv runs the most comprehensive curriculum in the area.

DareDevil Improv Classes

Improv 101 - Foundations

Perfect for beginners! Learn the fundamentals of "yes, and," listening, and scene work. This 6-week course covers everything you need to start your improv journey.

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Price: ~$225
  • No experience required

Improv 201-404 - Advanced Levels

Continue your training through advanced scene work, long-form improv, and performance techniques. Each level builds on the previous, culminating in performances at Dead Crow Comedy Room.
